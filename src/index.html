studio history

I’ve been a product designer for the last 10 years. I focused on accessible Product Design in healthcare, and democratising processes and workflows for internal teams. I have a deep understanding of building design systems that enable teams to efficiently build accesible products.

In March 2022, I left the world of full-time Product Design and set up Sam King Studio. I wanted a space to take on freelance projects, and create personal work that explores more of my interests.

If you’d like to learn about my pre-studio work, check out samking.design.
